---
title: "Switching Companies"
description: "How to switch between multiple companies when you belong to more than one workspace."
---

Aourly supports multi-company access, meaning a single user account can belong to multiple companies. If you belong to more than one company, you can easily switch between them without logging out.

## How to Switch Companies

### Step 1: Click the Company Name

Look at the sidebar or header area of Aourly. You will see the name of your currently active company. Click on it to open the company switcher dropdown.

### Step 2: Select a Company

The dropdown lists all companies you have access to. Click the company you want to switch to. The page reloads and you are now working in the context of the selected company.

> **Tip:** All data you see -- clients, assignments, invoices, time entries -- belongs to the currently selected company. Switching companies changes your entire context.

## How Company Isolation Works

Each company in Aourly is a completely isolated workspace. When you are viewing a company, you only see that company's data:

- Clients belonging to that company
- Assignments created under that company
- Time entries logged for that company's assignments
- Expenses tracked for that company
- Invoices generated by that company
- Bookkeeping records for that company

## Roles Across Companies

Your role can differ from one company to another:

- You might be the **Owner** of your own company with full control
- You could be a **Coordinator** in another company, managing assignments and approvals
- You could be a **Performer** in another company, with access limited to logging time and expenses

See the [Roles & Permissions](roles-and-permissions) guide for details on what each role allows.

## Things to Keep in Mind

- **Data is isolated** -- You cannot copy or transfer data between companies
- **Notifications are per-company** -- You may receive notifications from all companies you belong to
- **Last active company** -- When you log in, you are taken to the last company you were active in
- **Subscription is per-company** -- Each company has its own subscription plan and billing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I see data from multiple companies at once?

No. Aourly shows data for one company at a time. Switch between companies using the tenant menu to view each workspace.

### Do I need separate login credentials for each company?

No. Your single Aourly account gives you access to all companies you belong to.

### When would I need multiple companies?

Common scenarios include running a side business alongside your main consultancy, subcontracting for a client's company, or transitioning from one company structure to another.


<Info>Each company in Aourly is a completely separate workspace. Clients, assignments, time entries, and invoices do not overlap between companies.</Info>
