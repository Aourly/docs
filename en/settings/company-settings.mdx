Company settings control the information and defaults that apply across your entire Aourly company. This includes your business details, branding, default rates, and uploaded documents. Only the company owner can access and modify these settings.

## Accessing company settings

In the sidebar, click your company name to open the tenant menu, then select **Company Profile** or **Settings**.

## Company information

This is the core business information used on invoices and other documents sent to clients. Fill in all fields accurately:

### Name

Your official company name as registered with Bolagsverket (the Swedish Companies Registration Office). This appears on all client-facing documents.

### Organization number (organisationsnummer)

Your Swedish organization number. For sole proprietors (enskild firma), this is typically your personal identity number (personnummer). For limited companies (AB), it is the company's registration number.

Getting this right is important -- it appears on every invoice and is required for valid Swedish business documents.

### VAT number (momsregistreringsnummer)

Your Swedish VAT registration number, formatted as SE followed by your organization number and a two-digit suffix (for example, SE5501011234-01). This is required on invoices if you are VAT-registered.

### Address, phone, and email

Your company's business address, phone number, and contact email. These appear on invoices and are used for client correspondence.

## Branding

### Company logo

If your subscription plan supports it, you can upload a company logo that appears on invoices and other client-facing documents. This adds a professional touch to your communications.

For best results:

- Use a high-resolution image (at least 300px wide)
- PNG or SVG with a transparent background works best
- JPG is also supported but may not look as clean on white backgrounds

Logo upload is available on paid plans. During the free trial, all other company settings are available.

## Defaults

### Default hourly rate

Set the hourly rate that new assignments start with. You can override this on each individual assignment, but having a sensible default saves time when creating new work.

### General markup percentage

If you mark up expenses when billing clients (for example, adding a handling fee to subcontractor costs), set the default markup percentage here. Individual assignments can override this value.

### Reporting frequency

Choose how often timesheets should be submitted. This sets the default for new assignments and helps your team know when time reports are expected.

## Company documents

Upload important documents that you share with clients or need for your business operations:

- **Terms and conditions** -- your standard contract terms
- **Insurance certificates** -- proof of professional liability or other insurance
- **NDAs** -- non-disclosure agreements for sensitive projects

These documents can be attached to assignments and shared with clients directly through Aourly.

## What these settings affect

| Setting               | Where it appears                              |
|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Company name          | Invoices, reports, client-facing documents     |
| Organization number   | Invoices                                       |
| VAT number            | Invoices                                       |
| Address               | Invoices, company profile                      |
| Logo                  | Invoices, company profile                      |
| Default hourly rate   | New assignment creation form                   |
| Markup percentage     | Expense calculations on new assignments        |
| Reporting frequency   | New assignment defaults                        |

Changing any of these settings does not retroactively update existing invoices or assignments. Only new records use the updated values.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can coordinators or performers change company settings?**
No. Only the company owner can access and modify company settings.

**Does changing the company name update existing invoices?**
No. Previously sent invoices keep the company name used at the time of creation. New invoices will use the updated name.

**Can I change the default hourly rate after creating assignments?**
Yes. Changing the default only affects newly created assignments. Existing assignments retain their individually set rates.

**What file formats are supported for the logo?**
PNG, JPG, and SVG are supported. PNG or SVG with a transparent background is recommended.

**Is the VAT number required?**
It is required if your company is registered for VAT (moms). If you are not VAT-registered, you can leave this field empty and your invoices will not include VAT.
