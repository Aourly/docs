Aourly includes a built-in todo system that helps you keep track of tasks, deadlines, and pending actions. Some todos you create yourself, others Aourly creates automatically when something needs your attention.

## Accessing your todos

Click **Todos** in the main sidebar to open the task overview. This page lists all your todos, both manually created and system-generated, organized by status.

## Todo types

### Manual todos

These are tasks you create yourself. Use them for anything you need to remember: sending a proposal, following up with a client, reviewing a contract, or any other business task.

### Automatic todos

Aourly generates todos automatically when certain events happen in the system:

- **Gig approval reminders** -- when an gig is waiting for client approval
- **Invoice review reminders** -- when an invoice needs to be reviewed or is overdue
- **Time review reminders** -- when a timesheet needs to be reviewed and approved
- **Overdue invoice alerts** -- when a client has not paid on time

These automatic todos ensure that important deadlines and pending actions are never overlooked.

## Creating a todo

1. Open the **Todos** page from the sidebar
2. Click **Create** to open the creation form
3. Enter a title for the task (keep it short and actionable)
4. Optionally add a description with more details
5. Set a due date if the task has a deadline
6. Save the todo

Good titles are specific and action-oriented. "Send Q1 invoice to Acme" is better than "Invoice stuff."

## Todo statuses

Every todo has one of three statuses:

| Status      | Meaning                                        |
|-------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Open        | The task is active and needs attention          |
| Completed   | The task has been finished                      |
| Cancelled   | The task is no longer relevant and was skipped  |

You can change a todo's status at any time. Completed and cancelled todos are kept in the system for your records.

## Filtering and organizing

Use the status filters on the Todos page to show only the tasks you care about right now. For example, filter to **Open** to see only active tasks, or look at **Completed** to review what you have accomplished.

## Todos attached to records

Todos are not just a standalone list. They can be connected to specific records in Aourly:

- **Gigs** can have related todos (for example, "Get client approval for website project")
- **Invoices** can have related todos (for example, "Follow up on unpaid invoice #1042")
- **Timesheets** can have related todos (for example, "Review March timesheet before invoicing")

When you view an gig, invoice, or timesheet, you can see its related todos directly on that page. This keeps tasks in context with the work they relate to.

## Dashboard widget

Your dashboard includes a todo widget that shows upcoming and overdue tasks. This gives you a quick overview of what needs attention as soon as you log in, without having to navigate to the Todos page.

The widget prioritizes:

1. Overdue todos (past their due date)
2. Todos due today
3. Upcoming todos (due in the near future)

## Tips for effective task management

- **Check your todos daily.** A quick glance at the dashboard widget takes seconds and keeps you from missing deadlines.
- **Act on automatic todos promptly.** They usually represent actions that are blocking other workflows, like an invoice that cannot be sent until you review it.
- **Use specific titles.** "Follow up with Anna at TechCorp about March invoice" is more useful than "Follow up."
- **Close completed tasks.** Marking todos as completed keeps your open list focused on what actually needs doing.

## Frequently asked questions

**What types of automatic todos does Aourly create?**
The system creates todos for pending gig approvals, invoice reviews, timesheet reviews, and overdue invoices. Each todo is created for the person responsible for taking action.

**Can I assign a todo to someone else?**
Todos are personal. Each user sees their own todos. Automatic todos are assigned to the person who needs to take the action.

**Do completed todos get deleted?**
No. Completed and cancelled todos remain in the system for your records. Use the status filter to hide them from your active view.

**Can I set reminders?**
Todos with due dates appear in your dashboard widget as they approach or become overdue. The system surfaces the most urgent tasks automatically.
