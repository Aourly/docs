Worknode is an umbrella employer -- in Swedish, an **egenanställningsföretag**. It lets you work as a freelancer without the overhead of starting and managing your own company. Worknode formally employs you, handles your salary, taxes, pension, and insurance, while you focus on finding clients and doing the work.

## The problem Worknode solves

In Sweden, if you want to invoice clients for your work, you typically need a business entity: an **aktiebolag (AB)** or an **enskild firma**. Both come with administrative obligations -- bookkeeping, tax declarations, employer contributions, VAT reporting, and more.

For someone who freelances occasionally, or who simply does not want to deal with business administration, these obligations can be disproportionate to the work itself.

Worknode removes that barrier. You work, you invoice through Aourly, and Worknode takes care of everything else.

## How it works

The arrangement has three parts:

1. **You** find your own clients and perform the work, just like any freelancer
2. **Aourly** provides the platform where you manage clients, track time, create invoices, and handle day-to-day business operations
3. **Worknode** is your formal employer, handling salary payments, income tax, employer contributions (arbetsgivaravgifter), pension, and insurance

When your client pays an invoice, the money goes to Worknode's client funds account (klientmedelskonto). They deduct their service fee, employer contributions, income tax, and any pension contributions, then pay the remaining amount as your net salary.

## What Worknode handles

Worknode takes on all the employer responsibilities that you would otherwise need to manage yourself:

- **Employment contract** -- you are formally employed, with the legal protections that come with it
- **Salary payments** -- your earnings are paid as a regular salary to your bank account
- **Income tax (inkomstskatt)** -- Worknode withholds and remits your preliminary tax to Skatteverket
- **Employer contributions (arbetsgivaravgifter)** -- the mandatory ~31.42% payroll tax that funds social security
- **Pension contributions (tjänstepension)** -- optional pension savings deducted from your salary
- **Insurance coverage** -- employer insurance through Worknode's organization
- **Reporting to Skatteverket** -- all tax declarations and employer reporting are handled for you

## Who is it for?

Worknode is a good fit if you are:

- A freelancer or consultant who does not have your own company
- Someone who wants to try freelancing before committing to registering a business
- A contractor who prefers the simplicity of being employed over self-employment
- Someone who values employment benefits like pension and insurance without managing them yourself

If you already have an AB or enskild firma and are comfortable with the bookkeeping, you can use Aourly directly without Worknode.

## Benefits

### No company registration

You do not need to register with Bolagsverket or Skatteverket as a business. No F-skatt application, no VAT registration -- Worknode handles all of that.

### No bookkeeping responsibility

Worknode manages the accounting. You do not need an accountant or bookkeeping software (beyond what Aourly provides for your invoicing).

### Employment benefits

As a Worknode employee, you receive:

- **Pension contributions** (configurable percentage)
- **Insurance coverage** through Worknode's employer insurance
- **Social security benefits** (sjukpenning, föräldrapenning) through the employer contributions Worknode pays

### Professional invoicing

Through Aourly, your invoices look professional and include all the legally required information. Clients see a proper business document with correct formatting and payment details.

## Plans

Worknode offers two plan tiers:

- **Flex** -- suited for freelancers with variable or lower invoicing volumes. Includes a different fee structure optimised for flexibility.
- **Pro** -- suited for freelancers with higher or more consistent invoicing volumes. Typically offers a lower percentage fee per invoice.

Each plan has different service fee percentages and may include different levels of service. When you register through Worknode, you select a plan that matches your expected invoicing volume and needs. You can discuss plan options during the registration process or contact Worknode through the errands system.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need F-skatt to use Worknode?**
No. Worknode has F-skatt. You are employed by Worknode, so you do not need your own F-skatt registration. Worknode handles all tax obligations as your employer.

**Can I switch from Worknode to my own company later?**
Yes. You can create your own company in Aourly at any time. If you decide to register an AB or enskild firma, you can transition away from Worknode and continue using Aourly as a company owner.

**What fees does Worknode charge?**
A monthly package fee and/or a percentage of each invoice, depending on your plan. Worknode offers Flex and Pro plans with different fee structures suited to different invoicing volumes.

**What is the difference between using Aourly with my own company versus Worknode?**
With your own company, you handle taxes, bookkeeping, and employer obligations yourself. With Worknode, those are handled for you in exchange for a service fee.

**Is this legal and established?**
Yes. Egenanställning is a well-known employment model in Sweden, used by thousands of freelancers. It is a fully regulated form of employment.
