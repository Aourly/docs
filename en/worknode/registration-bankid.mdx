To use Worknode through Aourly, you need to register and verify your identity with BankID. This is a one-time process that confirms who you are and provides your personnummer, which Worknode needs to employ you and handle your taxes.

## What is BankID?

BankID is Sweden's electronic identification system, used by banks, government agencies, and many other services for secure digital identity verification. It works as a digital ID card on your phone or computer, and is trusted across the Swedish financial system.

## Before you begin

Make sure you have:

- A **Swedish personnummer** (personal identity number)
- The **BankID app** installed on your phone or computer
- An **active BankID certificate** (contact your bank if you are unsure)
- A valid **email address**

> **Info:** You need a Swedish personnummer and BankID to register as a Worknode user. This is a legal requirement for employment in Sweden.

## Step-by-step registration

### 1. Register for an Aourly account

Go to aourly.com and click **Register** or **Get Started** in the top-right corner. This begins the standard Aourly registration process.

### 2. Choose Worknode as your account type

On the registration page, select **Register via Worknode**. This tells Aourly that you want to freelance through Worknode's umbrella employment model rather than registering with your own company.

### 3. Verify your identity with BankID

You will be prompted to authenticate with BankID. Depending on your setup:

- **Mobile BankID**: Open the BankID app on your phone when prompted and confirm the authentication
- **BankID on computer**: Follow the on-screen instructions to authenticate using BankID on your computer

BankID securely provides your personnummer and verifies your identity. This step is mandatory -- Worknode operates under Swedish employment law and must verify your identity for tax and legal purposes.

### 4. Your personnummer is retrieved

After successful BankID verification, your personnummer is retrieved automatically. Some fields will be pre-filled from your BankID data (typically your name and personnummer). Fill in the remaining fields:

- Email address
- Phone number
- Any other required information

### 5. Create your account

Choose a strong password for your Aourly account and click **Register**. A verification email will be sent to confirm your email address. Click the link in the email to verify.

### 6. Account enters Pending Approval

Your account is now created with a status of **Pending Approval**. This means Worknode staff will review your application before you can start sending invoices.

You will be notified by email when your account is approved.

### 7. Worknode reviews and approves

Worknode staff review your registration to ensure all information is correct and complete. This typically takes 1-2 business days. Once approved, your account is fully activated and you can begin creating assignments and invoices.

## What you can do while waiting for approval

Even before your account is approved, you can:

- Log in to Aourly and explore the platform
- Set up your Worknode profile (bank details, tax settings, pension preferences)
- Create and manage clients
- Browse available features and familiarise yourself with the interface

> **Warning:** While your account is pending approval, you can still set up your profile and create clients, but you cannot send invoices until approved.

## What you cannot do until approved

- Send invoices to clients
- Perform financial transactions
- Use features that require an active Worknode employment status

## Troubleshooting

**BankID app not responding**
Make sure the app is updated to the latest version and that your phone has an internet connection.

**Certificate expired**
Contact your bank to renew your BankID certificate. This is separate from the BankID app itself.

**Verification fails repeatedly**
Try restarting the BankID app. If the problem persists, contact your bank to check the status of your BankID.

**No verification email received**
Check your spam or junk folder. If the email has not arrived after a few minutes, try requesting a new verification email from the login page.

## Frequently asked questions

**What if I don't have BankID?**
BankID is required for Worknode registration. You can get BankID through your Swedish bank. Visit your bank's website or contact them to set up Mobile BankID.

**How long does approval take?**
Typically 1-2 business days. Worknode staff review each registration to ensure all information is correct.

**Can I register without a Swedish personnummer?**
No. BankID requires a Swedish personnummer, and Worknode operates under Swedish employment law which requires a valid personal identity number.

**What if I already have an Aourly account?**
If you registered previously with your own company, contact Aourly support to discuss adding Worknode to your existing account.
