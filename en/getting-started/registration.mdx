Creating your Aourly account is the first step toward managing your freelance business with ease. The process takes just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Go to the Registration Page

Navigate to the Aourly website and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner, or go directly to the registration URL. You will be taken to the registration form.

## Step 2: Enter Your Details

Fill in the registration form with the following information:

- **Name** -- Your full name as you want it to appear in the platform
- **Email** -- A valid email address that you have access to. This will be your login identifier
- **Password** -- Choose a secure password with at least 8 characters

> **Tip:** Use a strong password that includes a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. A password manager can help you generate and store a strong, unique password.

## Step 3: Submit the Form

Click the **Register** button to create your account. Aourly will send a verification email to the address you provided.

## Step 4: Verify Your Email

Open your email inbox and look for a message from Aourly. Click the **verification link** in the email to confirm your email address and activate your account.

> **Tip:** If you do not see the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. Some email providers filter automated messages aggressively.

## Step 5: Create Your First Company

Once your email is verified, Aourly will guide you through creating your first company. You will need your company name, organization number, and other business details. See the [Creating a Company](../company/creating-a-company) guide for full details.

## Invited Users

If you received an invitation link from someone who already uses Aourly, click that link instead of registering through the homepage. The invitation will guide you through a simplified registration and automatically connect you to their company with the role they assigned.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use Aourly without a Swedish company?

Yes, Aourly supports companies in multiple countries. However, some features like Fortnox integration and BankID verification are specific to Swedish businesses.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes, new companies receive a free trial period to explore all features before choosing a subscription plan. No credit card is required to start the trial.

### What if I already received an invitation to join a company?

Click the invitation link in the email you received. You will be guided through registration and automatically added to the company that invited you.

### I get an error saying my email is already registered

This means an account already exists with that email address. Try logging in instead, or use the [Password Reset](password-reset) flow if you have forgotten your credentials.
