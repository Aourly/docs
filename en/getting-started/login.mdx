Once you have registered and verified your email, you can log in to Aourly to access your company dashboard and start working.

## Step 1: Go to the Login Page

Navigate to **aourly.com** and click the **Log In** button in the top-right corner, or go directly to the login URL if you have it bookmarked.

## Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

On the login page, enter:

- **Email** -- The email address you registered with
- **Password** -- The password you chose during registration

> **Tip:** Use a password manager to autofill your credentials securely and save time on repeated logins.

## Step 3: Click Log In

Click the **Log In** button. If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to your company dashboard.

### Remember Me

Check the **Remember me** option to stay logged in on trusted devices. This keeps your session active for a longer period, so you do not need to enter your credentials every time you visit Aourly.

> **Tip:** Only use the "Remember me" option on personal or trusted devices. Avoid using it on shared or public computers.

## After Logging In

When you log in, Aourly takes you to the dashboard of the last company you were active in. From here you can:

- View your time tracking stats and upcoming tasks
- Use quick actions to create clients, gigs, time entries, expenses, or invoices
- Navigate to any section using the sidebar menu

If you belong to multiple companies, use the tenant switcher in the header to change companies. See the [Switching Companies](../company/switching-companies) guide for details.

## Logging Out

To log out of Aourly:

1. Click your **profile avatar** or name in the bottom-left of the sidebar
2. Select **Log Out** from the menu

Always log out when using a shared or public computer to protect your account and business data.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to start the password reset process. See the [Password Reset](password-reset) guide for step-by-step instructions.

### Can I stay logged in?

Yes, check the **Remember me** option on the login form to keep your session active for longer. For security, sessions will still expire after an extended period of inactivity.

### I am locked out of my account. What should I do?

If you have tried logging in multiple times with incorrect credentials, your account may be temporarily rate-limited. Wait a few minutes and try again. If you still cannot access your account, use the [Password Reset](password-reset) flow or contact support.
