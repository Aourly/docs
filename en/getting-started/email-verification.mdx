After registering for Aourly, you need to verify your email address before you can fully use the platform. This is a one-time step that ensures your account is secure and that important notifications reach you.

## Why Verification Is Required

Email verification serves several important purposes:

- **Account security** -- Confirms that you control the email address linked to your account
- **Invoice delivery** -- Aourly sends invoices and payment confirmations via email
- **Notifications** -- Time-sensitive alerts about approvals, payments, and assignments are sent to your email
- **Password recovery** -- If you ever need to reset your password, the reset link goes to your verified email

Without verification, your access to Aourly features is restricted.

## Step 1: Find the Verification Email

After completing registration, Aourly automatically sends a verification email to the address you provided. Open your email inbox and look for a message from Aourly.

> **Tip:** The email typically arrives within seconds, but may take up to a few minutes depending on your email provider. If you cannot find it, check your **spam or junk folder** -- some email providers filter automated messages.

## Step 2: Click the Verification Link

Open the email and click the **Verify Email Address** button or link. This takes you back to Aourly and confirms your email ownership.

## Step 3: Continue Setup

After verification, you will be redirected into Aourly where you can continue with creating your company and getting started with the platform.

## Resending the Verification Email

If you did not receive the email or the link has expired, you can request a new one:

1. Go to the Aourly login page and log in with your email and password
2. You will see a verification required screen with a message asking you to check your email
3. Click the **Resend Verification Email** button
4. Check your inbox again (including spam/junk) for the new email

> **Tip:** You can resend the verification email multiple times if needed. Each new request invalidates previous links, so only the most recent link will work.

## Verification Link Expiration

For security, verification links expire after a set period. If you click an expired link, you will see a message indicating it is no longer valid. To fix this, log in and click **Resend Verification Email** to get a fresh link.

## Common Issues

### Email never arrives

If you have waited several minutes, checked spam, and resent the email:

- Make sure the email address in your Aourly account is correct and does not contain typos
- Some corporate email systems have delayed delivery -- wait up to 15 minutes
- Your email provider may be blocking messages from Aourly's sending domain
- Contact Aourly support if nothing else works

### Wrong email address

If you registered with the wrong email address and cannot receive the verification, you may need to create a new account with the correct email address or contact support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is email verification required?

Email verification confirms you own the address and ensures you will receive important notifications about invoices, payments, and account security.

### How do I resend the verification email?

Log in to Aourly. You will see a banner prompting you to verify your email with an option to resend the verification link.

### Can I change my email address before verifying?

You will need to contact support or register a new account with the correct email address.

### The verification link expired. What now?

Log in and click the "Resend verification email" option. A new link will be sent to your email.
